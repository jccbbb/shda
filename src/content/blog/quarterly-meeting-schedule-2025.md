We are thrilled to announce the complete schedule for SHDA's quarterly meetings in 2025. These gatherings are the cornerstone of our collaborative approach, bringing together passionate hard dance organizers from across Scandinavia.

## 2025 Meeting Schedule

### Q1 Meeting - Stockholm, Sweden
**Date:** March 15, 2025
**Location:** Stockholm, Sweden
**Host:** Forced 2 Dance

Our first meeting of the year will focus on planning the spring and summer event season, coordinating collaborative bookings, and reviewing our strategic goals for 2025.

### Q2 Meeting - Copenhagen, Denmark
**Date:** June 20, 2025
**Location:** Copenhagen, Denmark
**Host:** DAP Events

Mid-year review and planning session, with special focus on resource sharing optimization and marketing initiatives.

### Q3 Meeting - Oslo, Norway
**Date:** September 12, 2025
**Location:** Oslo, Norway
**Host:** EFFEKT

Autumn planning meeting to coordinate the busy fall and winter season, including year-end events and holiday celebrations.

### Q4 Meeting - Helsinki, Finland
**Date:** December 5, 2025
**Location:** Helsinki, Finland
**Host:** Hardstyle DNA

Our annual meeting includes elections, financial review, and strategic planning for 2026.

## What Happens at Quarterly Meetings?

Each quarterly meeting follows a structured format designed to maximize value for all members:

- **Updates & Reports:** Members share recent successes, challenges, and upcoming plans
- **Collaborative Planning:** Identify opportunities for joint events and shared bookings
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Presentations on best practices and industry insights
- **Resource Coordination:** Discuss infrastructure, equipment, and logistics sharing
- **Networking:** Informal networking and relationship building among members

## Attendance

All SHDA members are encouraged to attend these meetings. Remote participation options are available for those who cannot attend in person.

For more information about attending meetings, please contact [board@shda.org](mailto:board@shda.org).
